Conference Location:

 

The Arlington Convention Center

1200 Ball Park Way, Arlington, TX 76011



Directions from Fort Worth (Interstate 30)

East on I-30 to the Baird Farm/ Legends Way/Ballpark Way exit #28 onto Copeland Road. Turn right onto Convention Center Dr. Take another right onto Ballpark Way. The Convention Center is located on the right just south of the Sheraton Hotel and north of the Ballpark in Arlington. Enter at the marquee and proceed to the parking area on the west side of the facility.

Directions from Dallas (Interstate 30)

West on I-30 to Ballpark Way exit. At the top of the exit, turn right. This will take you back over I-30 (south bound). The Convention Center is located at the bottom of the hill on the right, just south of the Sheraton Hotel and north of the Ballpark in Arlington. Enter at the marquee and proceed to the parking area on the west side of the facility.

Directions from DFW Airport (Mid-Cities Area)

South on Highway 360 to the Lamar/Six Flags Drive exit. Turn right on Lamar (first light). At the third traffic light, turn left onto Ballpark Way. Cross over I-30 and go through one light.  The Convention Center is located on the right just south of the Sheraton Hotel and north of the Rangers Ballpark in Arlington. Enter at the marquee and proceed to the parking area on the west side of the facility.
