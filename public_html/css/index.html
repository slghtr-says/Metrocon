July 1st - From the Desk of the MetroCon Chair

Greetings,

Let me take the time to introduce myself to you. My name is Timothy O’Conner, IEEE 2013 MetroCon Chair. On behalf of the IEEE Fort Worth Section, I would like to invite you to attend our 26th Annual IEEE MetroCon. The IEEE 2013 MetroCon will take place at the Arlington Convention Center on Wednesday, October 09, 2013. The conference will feature vendor exhibits, Student poster contest, plenary session, seven track sessions consisting of 5 speakers each, two hour keynote luncheon, and post reception. The speakers will come from industry and academia. The participants come from the Dallas Fort Worth Metroplex which includes Fort Worth, Arlington, Grand Prairie, and Dallas. The conference presents networking opportunities for technical professionals in engineering, engineering graduate and undergraduate students, and university representatives (including instructors).

This year’s conference will kick-off with a plenary session at 7:30 a.m. The plenary session will follow with the first half of the track session which will commence at 8:30 a.m. and end at noon. At noon we will start our keynote luncheon which will include a motivational speaker and keynote address from Lockheed Martin (one of the major sponsors). The luncheon will be followed by the afternoon track sessions that will start at 2:00 p.m. and end at 4:45 p.m. The entire conference will conclude with a post conference reception. At the post conference reception will we offer giveaways in terms of door prizes for our conferees. Last year approximately 400 attendees representing technical professionals, college students, university professors (UTA, UNT, UNTHSC, and TCU), and industry representatives (BAE, Tektronix, Agilent Technologies, Thunderbird Technologies, Oncor, Devicix, Lockheed Martin, and Rhode-Schwarz) attended the conference. Continuing education and networking opportunities were experienced by all who attended. The technical papers delivered by such outstanding track session track presenters were simply superb.

We are looking forward to increasing the attendance 25% this year to the level of 500. On-line registration will be available on our website: www.metrocon.org. on or before August 01. For the latest information about the 2013 IEEE MetroCon we invite you to visit our website regularly.

Can we count on you?

Timothy O’Conner

IEEE 2013 MetroCon Chair

June 15th - Welcome to Metrocon 2013!

MetroCon is an annual engineering conference hosted by IEEE-Fort Worth.  The conference's aim is to educate engineers in the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex on the latest developments in science, engineering, and technology.

A Comprehensive Engineering Conference

Metrocon 2013 will include six parallel tracks, and is already looking to include one of the strongest programs ever! Attendees will have the opportunity to rapidly grasp the technical and societal implications of some of the most promising and innovative new developments and ideas. Each track has been organized into well coordinated morning and afternoon sessions designed to achieve maximum exposure in minimum time.

Returning Tracks

  • Biotechnologies
  • Emerging Technologies
  • Developing and Deploying Secure Systems
  • Software and Systems Engineering
  • Cutting Edge Technologies
  • Power & Energy Systems
