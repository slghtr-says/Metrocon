MetroCon 2005
Wednesday, September 14th, 2005
8:30am-4:45pm

Wyndham Arlington Hotel
1500 Convention Center Drive Arlington, TX (on I-30 midway between Dallas and Fort Worth)

 


MetroCon 2002 takes place at the Wyndham Arlington Hotel on I-30 in Arlington, the same place as last year (see photo).  The Wyndham is located mid-way between Dallas and Fort Worth, immediately off of I-30 in front of the Texas Rangers Ballpark.  From both Dallas and Fort Worth, take the Ballpark Way exit.  DFW Airport is just 8 miles north of the hotel.  See map above for further directions, or the Wyndham Arlington Hotel website.  There is plenty of free parking space on the hotel grounds.
