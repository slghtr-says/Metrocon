MetroCon 2006 takes place at the Wyndham Arlington Hotel on I-30 in Arlington, the same place as last year. The Wyndham is located mid-way between Dallas and Fort Worth, immediately off of I-30 in front of the Texas Rangers Ballpark. From both Dallas and Fort Worth, take the Ballpark Way exit. DFW Airport is just 8 miles north of the hotel.  Click on map below for further directions, or visit the Wyndham Arlington Hotel website for directions. There is plenty of free parking space on the hotel grounds.
