Securing Converged Networks
Dr. Gheorghe Spiride
Nortel Networks

The presentation starts by discussing the need for building layered security defenses that are able to meet the security challenges and requirements of emerging converged networks. Next, I cover in more detail the characteristics of a unified security framework that addresses the specific security requirements of enterprise networks, and conclude with a case study of enforcing security in order to protect wireless CDMA users and operations.

Dr. George Spiride received a BSCS from the ``Politehnica'' University Bucharest, Romania in 1994, a MSCE in 1995, and a PhD in computer engineering from Southern Methodist University in 1999. He joined Nortel Networks as a Member of Scientific Staff, working on optical network design and planning, and later on designing wireless access OAM systems. Currently, he is a Senior Member of Scientific Staff in the Converged Multimedia Networks Systems Engineering group, working on wireless networks OAM performance and capacity. His research interests include optical network architectures, network design and planning, simulation, and performance evaluation. He is an ACM member and an IEEE affiliate.
