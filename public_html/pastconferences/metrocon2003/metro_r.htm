"Seeking Competitive Edge"
   September 23rd, 2003
In addition to this years keynote speaker, this year’s MetroCon 2003 offers three exciting tracks. Each track formally covers topics in the morning and then extends the afternoon sessions to provide sufficient time to study topics in depth.

The Enabling Technologies Track returns this year, building on its strong technology legacy. This year the track will continue to cover topics in Nano-Technology, as well as, Quantum Cryptology, Optical Sensing and Smart Antennas.

Software development and the Internet have continued to evolve. This year the Software / Internet Track will cover topics on Wireless Security and more.

The Engineering / Program Management Track is new to MetroCon this year. Technical Managers will have a chance to be informed on pertinent subjects presented by local, well known, management experts. In addition PMPs can earn Professional Development Units (PDUs) towards their certification requirements.
