Welcome from the Chairman

Welcome, one and all, to the 1998 IEEE Region 5 Conference web site! Throughout the coming months, I invite you to visit us at this site, as we put together an exciting program of events to meet your IEEE business, professional, and social needs. A strong group of volunteers is currently working hard to make this conference one of the most successful and enjoyable in the history of the event.

The conference will take place at the lovely Worthington Hotel in the heart of downtown Fort Worth over the April 16-19th weekend, 1998. The conference will include most of the traditional events, such as the business meetings and student activities, as well as a technical symposium on the Friday of the conference. Look out too for some exciting social activities as the conference coincides with the Fort Worth Main St. Arts Festival.
Stay tuned for all the details over the coming months!

Alan Triggs
General Chairman
